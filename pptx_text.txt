Problem Statement Decisions by vibes Teams pick models from vendor benchmarks or a handful of prompts — not measured evidence on their own data. No unified view Quality, faithfulness, latency, and cost are never compared together in one place. Leaderboards ≠ your use case Public scores ignore your domain, your documents, and your retrieval setup. Costly, undefendable choices The wrong model wastes money and erodes trust in the system. Choosing an LLM today is mostly guesswork. LLM EVALUATION PLATFORM
--- p5 ---
LLM EVALUATION PLATFORM Proposed solution — Two evaluation tracks RAG (auto-generated QA from your documents) and Open QA (Any CSV/XLSX questions set you upload). One dashboard Faithfulness, relevance, latency, and cost compared across every model. Dual scoring RAGAS metrics combined with an LLM-as-judge for nuanced quality assessment. Reproducible runs Each evaluation is stored and re-comparable — results you can defend. A deployed platform that benchmarks LLM outputs side-by-side on your data.
--- p6 ---
Project objectives Compare four models GPT-4o, Llama, DeepSeek and Qwen under identical conditions. Cover both modes RAG quality and open-ended factuality on the same platform. Expose trade-offs Surface quality vs. latency vs. cost for every model. Ship a real product A usable, deployed web app — not a notebook. Automate evaluation Repeatable pipeline with no manual scoring. LLM EVALUATION PLATFORM
